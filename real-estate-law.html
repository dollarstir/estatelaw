Real Estate Law Practice

Our firm specializes in advising clients, ranging from seasoned investors to first-time home buyers through all aspects of the residential and commercial real estate transaction processes. Whether you are buying or selling a house, condominium, co-op, or a mixed-use building, we can provide guidance and counsel through the intake, due diligence, contract negotiations, closing, and post-closing stages. The transactions we handle include purchases and sales of single and multi-family homes, condominiums, cooperative units, brownstones, commercial properties, as well as complex real estate development projects. From inception, our firm has been committed to remaining accessible and to maintaining the best client relationship experience possible.

Contact us today to assist you with any of the following real estate service areas:

  • Residential Closings (condominiums, co-ops, houses)
  • Commercial Closings (retail, mixed-use, etc.)
  • Sponsor & Investor Closings
  • Residential & Commercial Leases
  • New Development Projects
  • Tax Abatements and Exemptions (421a, j51, etc.)
  • 1031 Exchanges
  • Short Sales
  • Refinancing
  • Deed Conveyances
  • Broker Agreements
  • Mortgage & Lending Documents
  • Real Estate Disputes & Litigation
